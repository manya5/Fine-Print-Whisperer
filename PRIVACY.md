# Privacy Policy — Fine Print Whisperer

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

Fine Print Whisperer helps you understand Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
pages. This policy explains exactly what the extension does with your data.

## What runs locally (no data leaves your browser)

- **Auto-detection** of ToS/Privacy pages and the on-page warning banner.
- **The regex-based scan and keyword chat fallback.**

These features read the visible text of the page you are on and process it
entirely inside your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.

## What is sent to our backend (only when you act)

When you **click "Scan This Page"** or **send a question in the chat**, the
extension sends the **visible text of the current page** and (for chat) **your
question** to our backend service, which forwards it to an AI provider
(currently Anthropic Claude) to generate the analysis or answer. The response is
returned to your browser and displayed.

- We send a randomly generated, anonymous install identifier with these requests
  solely to enforce fair-use rate limits. It is not tied to your identity.
- We do **not** collect your name, email, browsing history, or any account
  information. There are no user accounts.
- Page text is used only to produce the immediate response. We do not sell data
  or use it for advertising.

The AI provider processes the submitted text under its own terms; see Anthropic's
privacy policy at https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

## Stored on your device

The extension stores only your settings (theme, auto-detect on/off) and the
anonymous install identifier in your browser's local storage.
